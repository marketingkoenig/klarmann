Scope of Services

Well prepared is half fabricated.

Sawing & cutting

We cut profiles, tubes and sheet exactly to size — individually or in series, as the first stage of our own production or as a stand-alone cutting service.

Dedicated to stainless

In our plant, stainless steel never meets carbon steel: processing dedicated to stainless, free of ferrous contamination — the foundation for parts that stay corrosion-resistant for good.

Machining

Turning and milling in-house: on our lathe and machining center we produce fitted parts, flanges and connection components — matched to the assembly.

Coordinated logistics

Delivered, processed, collected — or taken straight to the next fabrication step: you decide how much production to add.

In stainless steel cutting, a plant dedicated to stainless is not a detail — it is the foundation: even the smallest ferrous particles from tools that previously processed carbon steel will later cause flash rust and corrosion damage. At Klarmann, the entire production processes corrosion-resistant alloys exclusively — your material never comes into contact with carbon steel.

Alongside sawing and cutting, machining is part of our pre-fabrication: our lathe and machining center produce fitted parts, flanges and connection components. Combined with laser cutting and forming, we deliver the complete material package for your design — or take on the entire assembly right away.

FAQ

In brief.

What does “dedicated to stainless” mean in practice? add

Our entire production processes corrosion-resistant alloys exclusively. Tools, supports and production halls never come into contact with carbon steel — so your material stays free of ferrous contamination, the most common cause of flash rust.

Do you also supply turned and milled parts? add

Yes — on our lathe and machining center we produce fitted parts, flanges and connection components, individually or as part of an assembly.

Do you also deliver cut parts directly? add

You decide: pick up your parts, have them delivered, or have us process them further on site — from the cut blank to a ready-to-install structure.
