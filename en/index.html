Founded as a forge — family-run to this day

Friedrich Klarmann's forge and repair shop has grown into a mid-sized plant engineering company: today, in the third generation, we design and manufacture with 80 colleagues in Westerstede-Ocholt — with full manufacturing depth under one roof.
