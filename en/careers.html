Frequently Asked Questions

In brief.

Do I need a cover letter? add

No. Your name, phone number and preferred job — that is all we need for the first step. We discuss everything else in person.

I have no metalworking experience — can I still apply? add

Yes. As a production assistant (m/f/d) you can join us as a career changer — we train you on the job. And if you want more, you can keep developing with us.

How quickly will I hear back? add

Quickly — we usually call you within a few working days. No waiting for months, promise.

Can I come and have a look first? add

Gladly. When we meet in Ocholt we show you the workshop and the team — and a trial day can almost always be arranged.

What is the metalworker apprenticeship like? add

You learn on real projects in production, with further training options and a very good chance of staying on after your apprenticeship.

What happens to my data? add

Your details go straight to our HR team and are used exclusively for your application. You can find the details in our privacy policy.
