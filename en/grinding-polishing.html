Scope of Services

Surfaces made to spec.

Weld dressing

Joining is only half the job: we dress, blend and brush weld seams until both appearance and function are right — on single parts as well as on complete plants.

Ra 0.8 µm or better

If your product calls for a defined surface roughness, our experienced grinding specialists deliver 0.8 µm or better — mirror-polished, ground, satin or marbled.

Hygienic surfaces

For food, pharma and cosmetics we grind surfaces that are easy to clean and low in dead space — matched to your validated process.

Electropolishing

For even more demanding requirements we offer professional electropolishing — through partnerships proven over many years.

With stainless steel, the surface decides where a part can be used: in the pharmaceutical, cosmetics and food industries, vessels and components must be cleanable without residue — and documented surface roughness is part of every acceptance test. Our experienced grinding specialists deliver defined surface finishes of Ra 0.8 µm or better — mirror-polished, ground, satin or marbled.

Whether as the final step of our own fabrication or as a standalone service on supplied parts: we dress weld seams, level surfaces and grind complete tanks and assemblies. For the most demanding specifications we arrange professional electropolishing through long-standing, proven partners — and with subsequent pickling & passivation in our own pickling shop, the surface is chemically back in prime condition too.

FAQ

In brief.

What surface finishes can you guarantee? add

Our grinding specialists produce defined surface finishes of Ra 0.8 µm or better — mirror-polished, ground, satin or marbled. We agree the required finish with you before the job starts.

Do you also grind supplied parts? add

Yes. You can deliver single parts or complete assemblies to us — we grind on a pure contract basis, on request including weld dressing and subsequent pickling.

Do you offer electropolishing? add

For particularly demanding requirements we arrange professional electropolishing through partners we have trusted for years — you keep a single point of contact for the complete surface finish.
