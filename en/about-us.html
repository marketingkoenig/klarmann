Since 1948 · Family Business

From the forge fire to stainless steel.

Friedrich Klarmann's forge and repair business has grown into a specialist for stainless steel processing — today run by the third generation, with a team of 80 in Westerstede-Ocholt near Oldenburg, Germany.

Our History

Six chapters, one common thread.

1948

The forge

Friedrich Klarmann founds a forge and repair business — craftsmanship as the foundation, to this day.

1990

The switch to stainless

Rolf Klarmann brings stainless steel processing into the company — plants for the food industry follow.

1998

The GmbH

Klarmann Edelstahlverarbeitung GmbH is founded at the new site in Westerstede-Ocholt.

2007

The manufacturing depth

Expansion of the production technology — contract manufacturing becomes the second pillar.

2011

The laser

Investment in in-house laser technology; two new halls add 3,800 m² of production space.

Today

The company

One site, a team of 80: plant & tank construction plus contract manufacturing — in the third generation, for four industries.
