Scope of Services

Brought into shape.

Rolling & curving

Shells for tanks, pipe segments, cones: we roll sheet metal into exactly the round shape your design calls for — the foundation of our tank construction.

Bending, folding & flanging

Precise bend lines for housings, trays and structural parts — and flanged edges for heads and lids: dimensionally accurate and clean, ready for the next step.

Ready for welding

Formed parts continue seamlessly at our plant: clamped, tacked, welded and dressed — with no transport between trades.

Between the flat sheet and the finished structure lies forming: we roll tank shells, pipe segments and cones, fold housings and trays, and flange edges for heads and lids — exclusively in corrosion-resistant materials and with a precise fit for downstream processing.

Because forming at Klarmann is not an isolated step but part of a continuous production chain, you save interfaces: the laser-cut blank comes from our own plant, and the rolled shell moves into the welding shop without a single transport leg — leaving you with a component from one source, documented and dimensionally accurate.

FAQ

In brief.

Do you also roll conical shells? add

Yes — alongside cylindrical shells we also roll cones and segments, just as tank and plant construction needs them every day.

Can I have parts formed only, without further processing? add

Of course. Many customers use us as an extended workbench just for rolling or press-brake bending — delivered, formed, collected.

Why is stainless-only forming important? add

Tooling that has previously formed carbon steel carries ferrous particles into the stainless steel surface — a corrosion risk. At our plant, stainless steel never comes into contact with carbon steel.
