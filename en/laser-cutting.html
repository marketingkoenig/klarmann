Scope of Services

From sheet metal to assembly.

Precise & repeatable

Our laser cutting system cuts stainless steel contour-accurately and with minimal burr — with consistent quality from the first part to the thousandth.

Further processing included

Blanks rarely stay blanks: on request we bend, weld, grind and pickle right away — everything at one site, with no transport in between.

Fast quotes

Send a drawing or DXF, receive a quote: short paths between inquiry, cutting and delivery — even when time is tight.

At Klarmann, the laser-cut blank is the precise starting point of almost every fabrication: on our large-format laser cutting system with a 3,000 × 6,000 mm working area, we produce contour-accurate, low-burr stainless steel blanks — repeatable from the first part to the thousandth. The system runs in our own plant in Westerstede — operated by colleagues who cut stainless steel all day, every day.

For you as a customer, that means: send a drawing or DXF, receive a quote, get your parts — as a pure laser-cutting job or with further processing included. Because unlike a cutting-only service provider, we keep going at the same site: bending and rolling, welding, grinding and pickling — all the way to a ready-to-install assembly.

FAQ

In brief.

What sheet formats can you laser-cut? add

Our laser cutting system handles sheets up to 3,000 × 6,000 mm — so even large-format blanks for tank shells and cladding are possible in a single piece.

Is a DXF file enough? add

Yes — a DXF or a dimensioned drawing is all we need for a quote. On request, our in-house design office will also create the drawing from your sketch.

Do you also laser-cut small quantities? add

From single parts to series production: short setup times make laser cutting economical for small batches and prototypes too.
